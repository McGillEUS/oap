OAP LITE 2022

APRIL 28 > APRIL 29

LOWER FIELD

We're back in person!

The wait it finally over :)

This year exceptionally, getting into OAP is going to look a little different.
We will be having a mandatory registration form to minimize the line.
48 hours before the event itself, we will release the registration form for students and patrons to sign up.
The form is free, and two-tiered.
1. Early bird tickets guarantee you a spot in line between 11:30 and 1pm
2. General tickets grant you access to the line any time after 1pm, but unfortunately do not guarantee entry into the event.
There will be a separate form for each day.
You can find everything posted on our Facebook Page and the Event Page !
